Sponge Iron

Sponge iron is made by the direct reduced iron process by coal-based technology. The material used is iron ore/lumps/pellets, Coal and Dolomite. The direct reduced iron process can be further simplified as removal of oxygen from ore in the solid-state to metalize the ore.

Billets

Billets are made by mixing sponge iron scrape

Captive Power

It is made from a waste heat recovery boiler. The waste heat of the kiln is used to convert water into steam and in AFBC boiler byproduct of coal and coal is used in the generation of steam. This steam is fed into a turbine to rotate it and subsequently generator and power is made.
